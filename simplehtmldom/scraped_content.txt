<h3>Ok so I forget to update my package managers all the time – and I made a mess of some dependencies. So I put a stop to it by writing this shell script.</h3> <p>First off I started out just having iTerm do it automatically by giving the commands to update on start. This was pretty long winded and I didn’t want all that going off every time I opened up my terminal to do some work, which is getting to be all the time now.</p> <p>So what I needed was something to remind me to update and not automatically do it every time.</p> <p> <a href="http://laurenclark.io/a-shell-script-to-update-your-package-managers/#more-19" class="more-link" rel="dofollow"><button>Read post <span class="meta-nav">→</span></button> </a></p>
